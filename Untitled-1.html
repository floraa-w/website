student council

2018-2019 Social Media Manager

As a team, we plan and execute a large variety of events, fundraisers, and sprit days to promote school spirit to the student body. An elevated and positive spirit is critical for students as it enhances their experience and performance at school. Serving as the first social media director in our school’s history, I kept the student body updated on upcoming events through posting on social media and making digital posters. On top of learning about leadership and how to plan events, I got a grasp on how to effectively use online platforms to promote and engage our students in our activities.

mental health network

finance director

We strive to destigmatize and raise awareness for mental health. By designing monthly workshops, we educate students about different aspects of caring for their own and other people’s wellbeing. Some topics we explore include anxiety and self-care.As finance director, my responsibilities include managing funds, communicating with the accountant, getting reimbursements, and brainstorming ideas for fundraisers. I am currently leading our first sticker fundraiser where I need to organize the supplier, cost, designs, etc. I learned how to delegate suitable tasks to the people I’m working with.

WYNS

human resources director

As the Human Resource Director of the Wish Youth Network Society (WYNS), I am in charge of overseeing all the events and clubs in the Vancouver region. WYNS is an organization that fundraises for local hospitals and raises awareness for medical issues. Along with the Regional Manager, we supervise a total of three schools and ten schools executives

shadow cabinet

Youth Parliament is an opportunity for students to learn about parliamentary procedure while debating current issues. Youth Parliament was an opportunity for me to step out of my comfort zone and challenge myself to speak in front of a large audience.
